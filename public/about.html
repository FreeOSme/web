About FreeOS.me

FreeOS.me is an independent Linux distribution project focused on digital sovereignty, long-term stability, and practical daily use. It is built for people who want a clean, transparent system they can trust and maintain over time.

The project is Debian-based and intentionally conservative with core system changes: security, reliability, and reproducible releases come first. Every release aims to stay lightweight, privacy-respecting, and free of unnecessary vendor lock-in.

FreeOS.me is currently in active development. Release notes and the full changelog are published openly so contributors and users can track decisions, package updates, and roadmap progress with full context.
